## Title
Salesforce in the Snow: Navigating Backup and Recovery in Challenging Conditions

## Abstract
Salesforce is the backbone of many businesses, but what happens when disaster strikes? Is your data protected? In this presentation, we will explore the best practices for Salesforce data protection, including backup and recovery strategies that can help you navigate the challenging conditions that come with data loss. We will discuss the importance of regular backups and show you how to implement a disaster recovery plan that can help keep your business running, even in the snowiest of conditions. Whether you're a Salesforce administrator, developer, or user, this presentation will provide valuable insights on how to keep your data safe and secure.

## Learning Goal
The learning goal for this talk is to provide participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to ensure the safety and security of their Salesforce data and implement effective backup and recovery strategies.
